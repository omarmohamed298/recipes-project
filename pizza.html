The Best Pizza Recipe Ever

Description:

This is the recipe of my favorite pizza ever. I first made it in 2000. This is perfect for cooking for family.

Ingredient List:

  • Dough
  • Pepperonis
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions:

  1. Knead our dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce onto circular dough
  3. Add cheese on top of our tomato sauce
  4. Spread pepperonis onto our pizza
  5. Put the pizza in the oven at 350F for 30 minutes
  6. Let the pizza cool and enjoy
