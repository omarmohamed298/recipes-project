The Best Tacos Recipe Ever

Description:

These tacos are simple, fresh, and ridiculously tasty. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or feeding a crowd.

Ingredient List:

  • Small tortillas
  • Seasoned protein (beef/chicken/beans)
  • Onion, cilantro, lime
  • Salsa of choice

Instructions:

  1. Warm tortillas in a pan until soft and pliable
  2. Cook or heat your protein until hot
  3. Fill tortillas with protein
  4. Top with onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime
  5. Add salsa and serve immediately
